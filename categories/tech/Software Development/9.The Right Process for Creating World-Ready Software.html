Start early. This mantra pretty much sums up the secret to successful software globalization and localization, and it applies to everyone on the team: program or project managers, designers, artists, engineers, testers, translators, marketers, sales and support. World-readiness is everyone’s job. It should not be the responsibility of a separate group of people. You may of course have globalization, culturalization, or translation specialists on your team, and if you do, it’s important to make sure these individuals have a seat at the planning table.
